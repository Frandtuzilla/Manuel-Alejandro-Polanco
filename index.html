Meet Manuel Alejandro

Rethinking the Future: Bridging the Gap Between Opportunity and the Need to Grow.

I am Manuel Alejandro Polanco Larreal, a lawyer, political scientist, and passionate advocate for social justice and education as drivers of change. My academic background and specialization in social inclusion, leadership, and strategic management have allowed me to lead projects that transform lives.

My philosophy, "Rethinking the Future," is an invitation to build bridges between opportunity and need, with a focus on vulnerable groups, particularly the Hispanic community and young people.
